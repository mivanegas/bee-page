Mighty Little Pollinators

"Away in the meadow, amid the red clover, the funniest music goes over and over: The honey-bee singing his song to the clover. The words, they are set to a rhythmical tune, a rumble, and bumble and zoon! Zoon! Zoon! Zoon! Goes the honey-bee, over and over, humming his tune to the blossoming clover."
🌼 Gertrude E. Heath 🌼
